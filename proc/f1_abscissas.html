subroutine f1_abscissas(n, x)

************80

! F1_ABSCISSAS computes Fejer type 1 abscissas.

Discussion:

The interval is [ -1, +1 ].

The abscissas are the cosines of equally spaced angles, which
are the midpoints of N equal intervals between 0 and PI.
